" WE ARE READY "

-says klopp

Looking relaxed and sounding upbeat after a short summer break, Klopp told ESPN that he is planning for the present and the future after winning two of a possible four trophies last season. The former Borussia Dortmund coach who signed a new contract until 2026 earlier this year, also discussed his vision of Liverpool after he leaves the club. But with the new season set to start in less than four weeks' time -- and with a World Cup wedged in the middle, from Nov. 21 to Dec. 18 -- Klopp says that the season ahead will be a challenge on and off the pitch as several teams are ready to compete for honours. The Reds have 10 days' respite before opening their Champions League round-of-16 tie at home to Bayern Munich on September 19 and then close out the month with league fixtures away to Manchester United and at home to Watford.
